Heartlands.

Here is the consolidated Master Document for the Heartland Alliance, combining all the specific lore details we established without adding new filler.

---

📖 MASTER FACTION FILE: Heartland Alliance

Tone: High Fantasy with "Volatile Tech" (Steam/Hydro-punk, but no radio/telegraph).
Setting: A stable region bordered by Chaos Storms.

---

👑 I. The Highborn (The Nobility)

Species: Horses, Zebras, Donkeys.
Role: Landowners, Senators, Diplomats.

  • Politics: Masters of the "Whisper War"—political maneuvering, backroom deals, and weaponized etiquette.
  • The Code: They act as "Watchmen." Their one unforgivable sin is "The Sleeping Watch"—failing to warn their vassals of danger. They maintain massive bonfire/mirror signal systems to warn of storms.
  • Magic Policy: Strictly forbidden. Any foal born with magic is tithed to the Church.
  • Military Asset: The Wind Runners. Elite, light-cavalry scouts funded personally by the Lords to carry messages and patrol borders rapidly.
  • Sub-Factions:
    • Stallions (Horses): The Dukes and Senators. High and mighty, obsessed with lineage.
    • Stripes (Zebras): Southern socialites and duelists.
    • Mules (Donkeys): The "Old Guard"—treasurers and judges who are stubborn and incorruptible.

---

⚔️ II. The Marshals (The Military)

Species: Wolves, Coyotes.
Role: The Army and Police. They serve the Nation/Moon, not the Nobles.

  • Religion: The Cult of the Shattered Moon. They believe they failed to protect the Moon in ancient times and serve now as penance.
  • Culture: Warrior-Poets. Rigid discipline (no bloodlust/savagery). They love opera, poetry, and the hunt.
  • Structure: The Alpha Seven.
    • Leadership is decided by 7 Alphas: 3 chosen by Combat, 3 by Mind/Strategy, and 1 Prime Alpha who must defeat all 6.
  • The Moon-Blessed: Magic users form "Perfect Packs" of 12 (one of each affinity) to act as secret police and investigators. Incomplete packs ("Half-Packs") perform minor duties in shame.
  • The Rotation: Packs rotate duties (guard/collection). They get one week off twice a year and one month off every five years (used for tournaments/breeding).
  • Rogues: Wolves who leave the pack often become mercenaries or airship marines.
  • The Coyotes: Internal security (Town Watch/Customs). The Alphas intentionally allow them to be "corrupt" and take bribes from the underworld to gather intelligence.

---

🌾 III. The Horned Commons (The Farmers)

Species: Deer, Cattle, Sheep, Antelope.
Role: Agrarian Workforce and Pioneers.

  • Chaos Farming: They live on the dangerous border. They plant crops near Chaos Storms to use Time Dilation (growing a harvest in days), risking annihilation for high yield.
  • Culture:
    • Men: Whittlers (always making toys/tools), Beekeepers, and Brewers (Pine/Fir Spirits).
    • Women: The "Knitting Circles." Shadow councils that run village politics and arrange marriages via bake sales.
    • Festivals: Shadow Week (Harvest) involves massive bonfires and high-agility dancing to hold back the dark.
  • Magic: They rely on Hedge Wizards (unsanctioned mages selling storm charms), constantly hiding them from the Church Inquisition.
  • Communication: Bell-Lines. Mechanical tripwires on fences connected to town bells.
  • Sports: "Sky-Leaping" (High Jump) and "Hoof-Ball" (Peasant Polo).

---

🦫 IV. The Infrastructure (Labor)

Species: Beavers.
Role: Builders, Geomancers, Union Workers.

  • The Iron-Tail Lodges: Unionized labor. Surface lodges are pubs/halls; Deep Lodges are secret underwater societies controlling blueprints.
  • Magic: Geomancy. They use "Tail-Slams" for seismic communication and p;ace binding runes into stone.

---

🦦 V. The River Syndicate (The Mob)

Species: Otters.
Role: Smugglers, Logistics, Spies.

  • Vibe: Bayou Noir/Tavern Culture. Trench coats and superstition.
  • Magic: Hydromancy. Rare "Singers" can summon fog Tech/Tools:
    • Whisper-Pearls: Entangled pearls for underwater communication.
    • Dock-Grease: Alchemical grease making them frictionless..
  • Operations: They handle the "Wet Work" (kidnapping/smuggling) and run the "Sunken Roads" (underwater currents).

---

🐀 VI. The Golden Warrens (The Bankers)

Species: Rats.
Role: Merchant Princes, Lenders, Vice Lords.

  • Vibe: Yakuza / Desert Merchant. Robes, tinted goggles, beaded tassels.
  • Structure: The Strand. Leaders are "Gilded" (Gold tail-bands). Punishment is "Docking" (removing tail segments).
  • Business:
    • Legal: Banks and Bond Houses.
    • Illegal: Exotic Vices (Black-Comb Opiates, Spore-Drift Hallucinogens, Iron-Bile). They strictly avoid Flower Valley drugs.
  • Combat: Cane-Fighters using hidden blades.

---

🦝 VII. The Ring-Tail Guild (The Trades)

Species: Raccoons.
Role: Shopkeepers, Climbers, Middle-Class.

  • Vibe: Irish-Immigrant / Bohemian.
  • Roles:
    • Men: Climbers (Roofers/Painters), Barbers, Salesmen.
    • Women: Bohemian "Mother Earth" types running Tree-Farms and Bakeries.
  • Traits: Always wear sunglasses/goggles. Excellent climbers.
  • Underworld: They run "Tree-Stills" (Moonshine) on the southern border and fence stolen goods.
  • Religion: Worship "The Lady of the Lantern" (matches the Bats/Church).

---

🐭 & 🐿️ VIII. The Services (The Small-Paws)

Mice (The Engine):

  • Role: Clerks, Scribes, Secretaries.
  • Culture: High-energy "Tiger Mom" parenting. Live in "Hill-Hollows" (clean tenement burrows).
  • Legend: "Chipper," the Mouse who was "Gilded" by the Rats.

Squirrels (The Sciurini):

  • Role: Elite Butlers and Spies.
  • Vibe: "Alfred Pennyworth" meets Secret Agent.
  • The Secret: The Guild. They use a ritual with a hallucinogenic "Star-Acorn" to unlock Biomancy (superhuman reflexes/processing).
  • Network: The Whisper-Web. Butlers trade state secrets via gossip codes.

---

🦔 IX. The Quill-Kin (The Healers)

Species: Porcupines.
Role: Doctors, Alchemists, Morticians.

  • Traits: Immune to poison/alcohol (from volcanic origins). Slow, deliberate, prickly.
  • Status: "The Quill Corps." Neutral on the battlefield. Harming one is a war crime (Legend of "The Branded One").
  • Secret: They follow a Death Cult ("The Spark-Bearer"), guiding souls across the void using captured "Life-Sparks."

---

🦇 X. The Night Watch

Species: Bats.
Role: Couriers, Spies, Mystics.

  • Duty: Night-time surveillance and message delivery.
  • Tradition: Farmers leave them fruit offerings; they eat the moths and watch the dark.

---

☀️ XI. The Church of the Eternal Dawn

Role: State Religion and Regulator of Magic.

  • Dogma: Order through Light. Magic is Chaos and must be "Yoked."
  • The Tithe: All children born with magic (Noble or Peasant) must be handed to the Church.
  • Factions:
    • Clergy: (Horses/Raccoons) Political leaders.
    • Paladins: Fanatical armored warriors ("Swords of Dawn").
    • Inquisition: Secret police who hunt Hedge Wizards.
