The Sacred Groves

The Sacred Groves

Aliases: The Dragon Prisons, The Anti-Chaos Eyes, The 12 Anchors Tags: [Locations, Magic, Lore, Chaos]

"To enter the storm is madness. To reach the eye is peace. To break the stone in the center is the end of the world." — Warden Field Manual

1.0 Introduction: The Anchors of Reality

The Sacred Groves are the twelve geographical linchpins of the Shattered World. They are the sites where the First Wardens and the dying God The Engineer successfully imprisoned the twelve Magistars following The Shattering.

They are not just prisons; they are "Reality Spikes" that hold the drifting continents together against the flow of Aetheric Tension.

2.0 The Anatomy of a Grove

To the average adventurer, a "Chaos Zone" is a terrifying region of storms and death. But for those few who survive the journey to the absolute center, they find the Grove.

  • The Outer Shell (The Chaos Zone):
    • This is the region corrupted by the leaking influence of the imprisoned Magistar. Physics breaks down here according to the Dragon’s nature (e.g., gravity fails near Tiraton, time loops near Termhill).
  • The Boundary (The Storm Wall):
    • The point of friction where the containment magic clashes with the Dragon’s will. This is usually a violent, visible barrier—a wall of solid lightning, a dense fog of spores, or a shimmering heat haze.
  • The Core (The Sacred Grove):
    • Inside the storm is a zone of "Impossible Serenity." It is a perfectly preserved, beautiful ancient Fae city. Time has stopped here. The architecture is pristine, the gardens are blooming, and the air is pure.
    • Why? This sanctuary is powered by the "Anti-Chaos" energy of The Fae Elders who sacrificed themselves. Their light holds back the darkness.
  • The Cell (The Dragonstone):
    • In the center of the Grove lies the Dragonstone Prison. This massive crystal formation (often the size of a building) contains the physical form of the Chaos Dragon.

3.0 The Known Groves (The Prison Map)

While there are twelve, only a few are charted by civilized nations.

The Grove of the Glazier (Magistar Stagus)

  • Patron: Magistar Stagus (Magistar Stagus).
  • Power: Ordo (Endurance/Ice).
  • Location: The Frozen North (Bordering the the Ursine Hegemony).
  • The Zone: "The Stillness." A region of absolute zero where nothing decays, but nothing moves.
  • The Threat: The Ice-Bound, frozen zombies that animate only to stop movement.

The Grove of the Furnace (Magistar Carulkem)

  • Patron: Magistar Carulkem (Magistar Carulkem).
  • Power: Nexus1 (Fortitude/Heat).
  • Location: The The Scute Confederacy1 (Eastern Tropics/Islands).
  • The Zone: "Elemental Catastrophe." Spontaneous firestorms and flash-freezes.
  • The Paradox: The Grove itself is a temperate, endless summer garden, untouched by the inferno raging miles away.

The Grove of the Black Star (Magistar Termhill)

  • Patron: Magistar Termhill (Magistar Termhill).
  • Power: Omen1 (Intuition/Time).
  • Location: The Gilded Steppe (Roaming Grounds of the Eastern Hounds).
  • The Zone: "The Haunting." A region where the past and future bleed together. Travelers meet their dead ancestors or see their own corpses.
  • The Paradox: The Grove is a place of blissful amnesia. Inside, you forget your name, your pain, and your history, existing in a perfect, eternal "Now."

The Grove of the World-Weight (Magistar Tiraton)

  • Patron: Magistar Tiraton (Magistar Tiraton).
  • Power: Mass1 (Might/Gravity).
  • Location: The Crushed Coast (Near the The Iron Caldera).
  • The Zone: "The Heavy Lands." Gravity fluctuates wildly. Mountains float, and flat plains suddenly exert 10x gravity, crushing travelers to paste.

The Grove of the Gilded Solvent (Magistar Aurgenas)

  • Patron: Magistar Aurgenas (Magistar Aurgenas).
  • Power: Flux1 (Finesse/Change).
  • Location: The Shifting Delta (Border of the River Folk).
  • The Zone: "The Melt." Solid matter turns to liquid; water turns to stone. The landscape rearranges itself daily.

4.0 The Secret Keys: Who Wants In?

The Groves are the ultimate prize in the world’s silent war.

  1. The Grey Wardens (The Jailors):

    • They patrol the perimeter (The Chaos Zone). They know that entering the Grove risks weakening the seals further, so they fight to keep everyone else out.

  2. the Worm Cults (The Breakers):

    • They want to reach the center to break the Dragonstone. They believe releasing the Magistars will usher in a new age of power (or just end the suffering of the world).

  3. The Gypsy Flocks (The Accidental Reinforcers):

    • The Twist: They are the only ones who know the secret paths to get inside safely.
    • The Mission: They perform the "Ritual of Return" inside the Grove, hoping to bring The Fae back.
    • The Reality: Their ritual actually recharges the batteries of the prison (the Anti-Chaos field), unknowingly keeping the Magistars locked up tight. They are the world's accidental saviors.
